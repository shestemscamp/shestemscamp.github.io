Hi there! We are three high school students at Alameda High School who have a passion for STEM and education. In middle school, we didn't realize how cool STEM was and the importance technology has on the world which is why we developed She STEMs. We hope to show girls about the influence of technology, how they can use tech for social good, and help every girl feel confident and capable to make a change.


Interested in joining our team or getting involved? Contact Us

Who run the world?
Girls!

Our camp focuses on girls because of the serious underrepresentation of
girls in STEM and the lack of self-esteem in girls.

For 2 weeks at the Harbor Bay Community Center from 9am to 4pm, 20 girls will learn how to create cool projects with STEM! The camp will feature hands-on projects, inspirational speakers, and field trips! At the end of our camp, our goal is for each and every one of our students to feel confident and capable to follow their passion and understand how technology influences the world.

NOTE: There is a $50 fee for the entire 2 weeks, and students will need to bring their own lunch and computer. We are offering 5 scholarships to cover the $50 camp fee for students who may have financial difficulties, and can bring up to 5 computers for students who may need them. Payment will be paid through cash or check in person on the first day of camp, or if necessary, we can set-up a payment through Paypal.
