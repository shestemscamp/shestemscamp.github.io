Programs

Workshops

Held from January through May on Thursdays, Fridays from 3pm to 5pm during the week and occasionally Saturdays from 10am to 1pm at local public facilities near you for 20 middle-school students. Learn about hardware computing and developing your own project!

Camps

For 2-weeks in the summer, we host a group of incoming 6th-8th grade girls from 9am to 4pm, teaching them about hardware computing through robotics, field trips, and more! We're currently planning a 1-week camp during spring break in the Bay Area.

Events

In the past, we've supported events and hosted field trips. This year, we'll be hosting a 1-day hackathon and working with more companies to host more field trips in the East Bay.

*All programs are free with donations appreciated!
