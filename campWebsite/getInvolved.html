Why get involved?

  • Empower girls in your local community and be a role model
  • Grow your public speaking, leadership, and STEM skills
  • Get community service hours and recognition
  • Spread your passion for STEM!

Why She STEMs?

  • We uniquely focus on girls and hardware computing
  • We'll provide you the materials needed
  • We'll help you learn and provide the support needed via calls, in-person meetings, etc.

What kind of program can I run?

  • Any! From camps to workshops, we have a bunch of curriculum already set for you
  • You have creative freedom to develop your own chapter
  • We can brainstorm news ideas together too
  • These programs can be themed, focus on a specific topic, or anything you can imagine.
  • It can take anywhere from 2 weeks -4 months to set you up depending on the program, so fill out our form today!

What doesn't it take to be a chapter leader or mentor?

3 Simple Steps
  • Sign Up: Register your new chapter
  • Develop: We'll contact you within 48 hours
  • Implement: Organize your chapter or find one to support

Become a Chapter Leader!

Create a chapter in your community and spread the mission of She STEMs!

Become a Mentor!

Are you a professional or experienced student who wants to get involved?
As a mentor, you'll help inspire our students to pursue their dreams and spark interests in STEM. You may be on a panel session, mentoring a chapter, or mentoring a student 1:1. You are role models to our students an will inspire their future careers. During this journey, you'll become inspired by the creative ideas of students and help out your community.
